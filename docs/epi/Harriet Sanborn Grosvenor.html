      EPIGRAPH: Seest thou my home? 'tis where yon woods are waving, In their dark richness to the summer air; Where the blue stream, a thousand flower banks laving, Leads down the hills, a vein of light - 'tis there. My home! the spirit of its love is breathing, In every wind that plays across my track; From its white walls the very tendrils wreathing, Seem with soft links to draw the wanderer back.
