      EPIGRAPH: And, oh! when the glad waves foam around, And the wind blows fair and free, The health that we drank to the Outward-bound Will come back to their memory. Old friends will still seem near them, In their ocean-cradled sleep; And that dreaming thought will cheer them, Far away on the lonely deep. Then fill, while the mid-watch passes, Fill, the toast let it circle round, From full hearts and brimming glasses, And, hurrah! for the Outward-bound!
