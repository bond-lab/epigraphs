Our girl in the know says Hanna made a whirlwind tour of the boutique last week and walked away with stunning pieces by some of Lebanon's best up-and-coming designers, including some very revealing swimwear. Summer is just around the corner and it seems that Hanna for one intends to enjoy it in style.
